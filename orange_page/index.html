All About Oranges

Introduction

Oranges are a type of citrus fruit that are not only delicious but also packed with vital nutrients.

Nutrition Facts

Vitamin Amount
Vitamin C 53.2 mg (89% DV)
Fiber 2.4 g (9% DV)

Fun Facts

  • Oranges are the largest citrus crop in the world.
  • The color orange is named after the fruit, not the other way around.
