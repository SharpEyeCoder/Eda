Apple

Health Benefits

Apples are nutritious and may offer numerous health benefits. They are high in fiber and vitamin C and are also low in calories. Apples have been linked to a lower risk of heart disease and improved digestion.

Varieties

There are many varieties of apples, including Fuji, Gala, Granny Smith, Honeycrisp, and Red Delicious. Each variety has its own unique taste, size, and texture.
